About Our Institution

RATHTALA M.D. VIDYANIKETAN has been a cornerstone of education in Burdwan since 1968. For over five decades, we have been dedicated to shaping young minds and preparing them for a brighter future.

As a Bengali medium institution affiliated with the State Board, we provide quality education from grades 5 through 12, fostering academic excellence and character development in our students.
